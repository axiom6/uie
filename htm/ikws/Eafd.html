Enterprise Architecture

  • Stories: Marketing today has evolved into selling stories as a lifestyle
  • Exclusive: Every salesperson tells a story that opens with their exclusive.
  • Alignment: Align business and technology as a single discipline.
  • Big Picture: Enable the big picture with informed decision making.
  • Default: All organizations have an Enterprise Architecture by default.
  • Harvest: Begin with harvesting the key documents.
  • 5 Groups: Business, finance, technology, security and lifecycle.
  • Opportunity: Create opportunities and evaluate their risks and benefits.
  • Prioritize: Make and prioritize recommendations to senior management.
  • Maturity: The best way to fulfill mission and goals.
  • Success: Determine all contributions to the business success.
